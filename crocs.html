Do You Like Wearing Crocs?

The Object

This project was purposed for students to research a subculture. Learning everything about your client can make things a bit smoother if the designer knows who he or she is designing for. As a result, I chose surfers. The reason why is simply I did not know anything about this culture. So I wanted to learn as much as i can about them. Things such as favorite food, clothing style, hobbies (beside surfing), educational status, and more.
